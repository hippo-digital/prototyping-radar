## What is this skill?

Building wizard patterns involves creating advanced multi-step interactions with branching logic, progress indicators, and the ability to go back and change answers.

## Why is it important?

- **Complex transactions** – Handle services that require many steps
- **User guidance** – Help users understand their progress
- **Change answers** – Allow users to review and edit their responses
- **Task list integration** – Combine with task list patterns for longer services
