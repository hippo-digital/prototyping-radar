## What is this skill?

Navigation patterns involves implementing sidebars, footers, breadcrumbs, and other navigation elements that help users understand where they are and move through your service.

## Why is it important?

- **User orientation** – Help users understand their location in the service
- **Efficient navigation** – Allow users to jump to relevant sections
- **Accessibility** – Proper navigation landmarks help screen reader users
- **Complex services** – Essential for services with multiple sections or tasks
