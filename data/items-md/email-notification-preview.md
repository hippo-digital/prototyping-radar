## What is this skill?

Email notification preview involves creating pages that show what emails or letters would look like, using data from the user's session. This helps test email content and timing during user research.

## Why is it important?

- **Realistic testing** – Users can see what emails they'd receive
- **Content testing** – Test if email wording is clear and actionable
- **Journey completeness** – Show the full experience including notifications
- **Stakeholder review** – Get sign-off on notification content
